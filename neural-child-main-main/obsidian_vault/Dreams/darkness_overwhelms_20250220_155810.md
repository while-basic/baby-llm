# Darkness Overwhelms

## Dream Content
Waaahhh... *flails arms and legs*

Mr. Chris... *looks up with wide, unblinking eyes* ...can't see anything... *whimpers* It's all black... *shudders*

*Suddenly, a faint image of a face appears in my mind's eye*... Mommy? *reaches out with an invisible hand* Is that you? *starts to wail*

But then, the face starts to change... *gasp* it's not Mommy at all... *covers eyes* No, no, no! *cries louder*

*Suddenly, a bright light fills my mind's eye, and everything goes white*

When I open my eyes again, I'm back in the dark... *whimpers* But this time, I see a big, loud... * covers ears* ...NOISE! *covers face with hands*

It's too much! *starts to sob uncontrollably*

But then... *pauses, and for a moment, the noise stops* ...I hear... *gasp*... Mommy's voice. *perks up head* "Shh, it's okay, little one..." *smiles weakly*

*The dream fades away, leaving me with an empty, dark space*
