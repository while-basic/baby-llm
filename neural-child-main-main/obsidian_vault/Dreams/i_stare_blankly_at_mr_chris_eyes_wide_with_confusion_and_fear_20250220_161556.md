## Dream Content

*I stare blankly at the "image"* ...there's nothing to see... *stares harder*... no shapes, no colors, just... *shrugs*... nothing.

*I start to rock back and forth, holding onto the imaginary toy tightly*... I'm safe... I'm happy... *starts to hum a soft, soothing tune*
