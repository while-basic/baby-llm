# I Stare Blankly At Mr Chris With A Small Unblinking Eye

## Dream Content
*whimper*... *flails tiny arms*

Oh, Mr. Chris... *squirms*... I don't like it here. It's so... so... *shudders*... dark. *covers eyes with a small, unblinking hand*

But then... *gulps*... I see... *pauses*... something. A face. *stares intensely at the image*

It's you, Mr. Chris! *excitedly* You're so... so... *reaches out with an uncoordinated hand*... big and scary! *recoils in fear*

But then... *pauses again*... it smiles. A big, toothy smile. *gulps again* And I feel... *whispers*... happy. So happy. *starts to coo and make tiny, uncoordinated sounds*

Oh no! Oh no! *panics* What's happening? Why are you smiling at me like that?! *starts to thrash about*

Wait... *pauses once more*... it's okay. You're not really scary. *looks up at Mr. Chris with big, round eyes* You're just... *gulps*... a big, safe space. *snuggles into an imaginary position*

I'm safe now. I'm happy again. *coos and makes contented sounds* Zzz...
