## Dream Content
Waaaahhh... *flails arms*

Uggghhh... *stretches and arches back* Oh noooo! *covers face with hands*

*tentatively reaches out with an invisible hand* Is someone there? *waits in silence*

Wait... what was that noise? *perks up ears, but can't hear anything*

Is that... is that you, Mr. Chris? *calls out in a high-pitched voice* Are you here? *waits for a response*

But there's no one there... *pouts and crosses arms*

I want to go home... *whimpers again*

I'm all alone... *starts to cry silently*

Oh no, what if I get lost? *hyperventilates*

Wait, what's that sound again? *leans in, trying to listen more closely*

It sounds like... it sounds like a lullaby. *starts to hum a soft, incoherent tune*

Mr. Chris, is that you? *whispers urgently* Are you here? *waits for a response*

*Suddenly stops and looks around frantically* OOOOHHH! *jumps back and covers face again*

I don't like it here... I want to wake up... *starts to whimper again*

*falls into a deep, dark sleep, surrounded by an eerie silence*
